Challenge 1)

Mark and John are trying to compare their BMI (Body Mass Index), which is calculated using the formula: BMI = mass / height ** 2 = mass / (height * height) (mass in kg and height in meter).

Test Data:

  • Marks weighs 78kg and is 1.69m tall.
  • John weighs 92kg and is 1.95m tall.

Challenge 2)

Using the data from Challenge 1, display who has a higher BMI. The message is either "Mark's BMI is higher than John's!" or "John's BMI is higher than Mark's!"
